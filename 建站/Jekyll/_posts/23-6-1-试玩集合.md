#### 加入集合设置
7. 加入集合设置
```yaml
collections:
  tweets:
    output: true
defaults:
  # Journals
  - scope:
      path: "星座/白羊/_posts/_tweets"
      #output: false
    values:
      permalink: journal/:year/:month/:day/:title
      category: journals
```
无效。
